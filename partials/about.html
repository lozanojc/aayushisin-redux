Aayush Iyer does what's needed to solve problems using design, code, words & thinking. With over 10 years of experience across advertising, product design and journalism, he brings empathy, critical thinking and cross-cultural insight to every situation.

Prior to this, he's worked with Ogilvy Mumbai, and also served as a product manager at zapak.com, architecting their online gaming property.
